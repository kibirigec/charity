### Change the Colors (2 minutes)
Edit `src/app/globals.css` lines 4-9:
```css
--lavender-primary: #9b87f5;  /* Your color here */
```

Edit these files in `src/app/components/`:

- `Mission.js` - Mission statement text

1. Put logo image in `/public` folder

3. Replace "God's Gifts" text with your logo
